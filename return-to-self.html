Soft launch event • Arts District, Los Angeles

Return to Self

A community healing gathering designed to show what Finding Eden feels like in real life: calming, creative, emotionally honest, and built around real human connection.

This first event is about gathering the right people, creating a meaningful visual example, and giving donors, facilitators, sponsors, and the public something tangible to believe in.

Become a CollaboratorSupport the First Gathering
The experience

Hybrid format: movement, storytelling, creative reflection, and quiet space.

Arrival & settling

Guests enter a warm loft-style environment with soft music, seating, grounding details, and room to exhale.

Somatic movement

A gentle body-based practice that helps people reconnect with breath, sensation, and the present moment.

Creative reflection

Journaling, art prompts, or sensory creative stations that support insight without demanding performance.

Story circle & closing

Optional sharing, guided reflection, and a softer ending that helps people leave feeling more resourced than when they arrived.

Core facilitators

Somatic practitioners, movement guides, artists, breathwork facilitators, sound practitioners, storytellers, and trauma-aware space holders.

Volunteers

People who can help with welcome, flow, setup, supplies, hospitality, and keeping the environment calm and supported.

Sponsors & brands

Supporters who can contribute funds, materials, refreshments, or aligned brand support while being visibly connected to the launch.
