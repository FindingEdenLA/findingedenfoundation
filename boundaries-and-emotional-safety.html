Boundaries and Emotional Safety

Boundaries are often talked about like they are simple communication tools. But for many people, especially people with trauma, boundaries are tied to emotional safety, attachment, and fear.

They are not only about saying no. They are about whether someone’s body feels allowed to stay connected to themselves while still being in relationship with other people.
