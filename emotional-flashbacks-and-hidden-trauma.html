Emotional Flashbacks and Hidden Trauma

Not every flashback is visual. Some do not look like a memory at all. They look like a wave of panic, shame, grief, helplessness, or emotional collapse that seems much bigger than the moment itself.

That is one reason emotional flashbacks can be so confusing. People think they are overreacting when often their body is reacting to something older than the present situation.
