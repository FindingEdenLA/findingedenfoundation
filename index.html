Where Stories Become Strength.

A place where people can slow down, tell the truth about their lives, and remember they are not alone.

Finding Eden is a community-centered healing initiative in Los Angeles creating spaces for storytelling, emotional support, creativity, movement, and connection so people can slow down, breathe, and feel human again.

Featured Banner

Return to Self — The first Finding Eden gathering in Los Angeles’ Arts District.

This first event is designed to show what Finding Eden could feel like in real life: a calming, creative, trauma-aware experience built around somatic movement, storytelling, creative reflection, and community connection.

We are currently looking for core facilitators, volunteers, sponsors, and early supporters who want to help shape the first workshop and be part of the beginning.

Founder Story

A place born from lived experience.

Almost every person I meet eventually opens up about something deeper than they planned to. Stress. Grief. Trauma. Old wounds. Confusion. Loneliness. Sometimes it happens with friends. Sometimes with strangers. Sometimes in quiet moments that somehow turn into honest conversations without warning.

A lot of people do not realize how much of their pain is still living in their body. They do not know how to slow their mind down, sit with themselves, or process what they feel in a way that actually helps.
